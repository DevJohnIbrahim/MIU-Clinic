International Dentistry Award

  Kreativ Dental Clinic are honoured to announce their latest award presented to them by GHP in June 2017 – International Dental Clinic of the Year 2017 - Europe.
GHP Awards Coordinator Rachel Devonport expressed her pride in the award winners: "Private healthcare is a crucial part of this already vital market, and as such the importance of those working tirelessly to provide excellence in this sector can never be underestimated.
As such I am honoured to be able to showcase the talent, dedication and sheer hard work of every one of my winners.
I would like to congratulate them on their success and wish them the best of luck for the future." Kreativ Dental Clinic beat similar clinics from throughout Europe to secure the prize

Kreativ Dental Voted No 1 Dental Clinic in Europe

  Budapest clinic Kreativ Dental wins judges commendation in major international award Kreativ Dental Clinic praised as ‘Highly Commended’ in International Dental Clinic category at IMTJ Medical Travel Awards 2015
Kreativ Dental Clinic has been lauded as ‘Highly Commended’ in a major international medical tourism award, for their efforts in attracting international patients to Budapest, Hungary.
Kreativ Dental Clinic was praised by the judges as part of the awards organised by medical travel publication The International Medical Travel Journal, (IMTJ, www.imtj.com) at their annual awards ceremony.
The awards were presented on 15th April at a high profile reception in London during the International Medical Travel Journal Medical Travel Summit, which brought together all the pre-eminent providers and experts in medical travel and medical tourism sectors.
The commendation was presented to Attila Knott, Owner and Managing Director of Kreativ Dental, who said:
“We have served patients and built this industry relentlessly for the last 15 years and we are proud to receive this prestigious recognition in dental tourism.” Attila Knott
